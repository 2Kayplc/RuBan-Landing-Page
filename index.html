About RuBan

The RuBan trans tech is a business setup to commercially secure the agricultural and logistics sector of the Nigerians economy with technology as its driving force. It is an online application that provides a digital space for farmers of all kinds of food products to upload their goods to a wide range of consumers and in addition, offers room for increased exportation of agricultural produce in the country.

On the other hand, we get to improve the lifestyle of thepopulation with a 'can't do without commodity' - Food, at its most affordable and healthiest condition. The end result of this goal provides:

1. An easy and comfortable means of agricultural trade.

2. Quick access to fresh foods .

3. A platform that fosters and increases exportation of various food products.

4. Affordable purchase of all kinds of foods and fruits.

5. A global connection between farmers and manufacturing companies.

6. Increase in the country's logistics and agricultural revenue sector.
